Yasmin's Story

My heart breaks every time I look into my children's hungry eyes. Sami, my 5-year-old, cries himself to sleep from stomach pains. Bassil, just 3, is so weak he can barely stand. My husband hasn't heard our voices in months since the bombs took his hearing. I feel so alone in this nightmare. Please, I'm on my knees begging you - help us survive!

Food Crisis

Yesterday Sami asked me "Mama, will we eat today?" and I had to lie to him. The hunger pains keep them awake crying all night. I can see their little ribs through their skin...

Medical Needs

My husband can't hear our children crying anymore. He can't work. He just sits staring at the wall, broken. The hearing aids would give him back his life - and maybe his will to live.
